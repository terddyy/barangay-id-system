Digital ID System E-Services
 
 A. Core Resident & ID Features
1. Resident Registration & Profile Management
Kapag may bagong residente, i-eencode ng staff ang pangalan, birthdate, address, at contact number.
Mag-a-upload din ng ID picture at i-link siya sa household records.
Lahat ng impormasyon ay naka-save sa centralized database, kaya madali hanapin kahit ilang taon na ang lumipas.
2. Barangay ID Generation
Gumagamit ng fixed standardized template para lahat ng ID ay iisa ang itsura at format.

3. Security & Verification (with AI Face Detection)
Ang system ay may role-based access (ibig sabihin staff lang ang puwedeng mag-edit).
Pwede ring mag-upload ng supporting documents bilang proof of residency.

Lahat ng activity gaya ng pag-edit, delete, at pag-print ay may audit logs.
4. Resident Registration Module (Bagong Core Feature)
Encode ng Full Name, Birthdate, Address, Contact Number.
Upload ng ID picture (auto-crop para eksakto sa standard size ng ID).
5. Fixed Template ID Generator
May barangay logo at naka-lock na design.

8. Database Storage

May login para sa authorized staff.

 B. Resident E-Services
- Dito may kasamang database. Ang database ay offline.
E-Document Requests – Residente maglo-login gamit ang ID number para mag-request ng barangay clearance, indigency, o certificate. May status update: Pending → Approved → Released.
Resident Record Control – Tanging verified residents lang ang pwedeng mag-request.
Complaint & Feedback System – Pwedeng magsumite ng reklamo o suggestion online.
Emergency & Health Services – Para sa health programs, relief distribution, at medical missions.
 
Event Registration & Attendance – Halimbawa, fiesta, clean-up drive, o barangay assembly, may registration at attendance tracking.
 
 C. Governance & Transparency
Financial & Transparency Features – Automatic nire-record ang mga bayad (hal. sa documentary fees).
Community Engagement Tools – Puwedeng mag-post ng announcements at events online.
 
D. System-Level Enhancements
System Security & Compliance – May data encryption para hindi basta-basta makuha ang impormasyon.
Governance & Access Control – Barangay captain/secretary may mas mataas na access, staff ay limited lang.
User Experience & Accessibility – Simple at madaling gamitin ang system, puwedeng mobile o desktop.
Deployment & Maintenance – Puwedeng i-install sa barangay server o cloud, at may regular backup.
 
E. Future-Ready & Smart Features
Integration & Expansion – Puwedeng i-connect sa ibang LGU system o national ID system sa future.
Performance Monitoring – Nakikita ang bilis at performance ng system, at may alert kung may error.
AI Human Face Detection & Verification – Siguradong tao ang nasa picture at tugma sa database.
AI Fraud Detection – Kung may suspicious activity (duplicate name, fake entry), automatic may warning.
AI Chatbot Assistant – Residents can ask (hal. “Paano kumuha ng clearance?”) at sasagot agad ang chatbot.
AI Analytics & Prediction – Gumagawa ng forecast gaya ng population growth, kailangan sa budget planning.
